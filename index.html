US. Department of Labor Redesign
Reverse the fustration, and accelerate the design function. Recreating a human-centered goverment agency website with fresh look.

Project Overview

Redesign the current US Department website to a productive content structure, increase positive accessibility experience for user while navigate through the site, and create a visual hierarchy and aesthetic presantation.With user-friendly and more human-centered UI element and organization structure, the overall experience to the government website can be lifted to it’s organiz purpose and optimize the mission of helping the public with work-related benefits and rights.

The problem

To improve the functionality and organization optimization of the website, resign the structure layout with more user friendly user interface components.

The Solution

Redesign the website to improve the functionality and accessibility of the website, restructure the layout with more understandable flow and user friendly interface components.

Annotation for current site

We did a 360 annotation throughout the website’s navigation and overall visual establishment on homepage and several other popular catagories. We found out that the site is mainly build on large scale of text content without white space and heiarchy and it’s generating the overwhelming feeling the longer you browsing through the site.

Brand Analysis

We looked at the current Department of Labor’s website and studied the importanceness elements in branding a goverment website. From there, we were able to construct the ideas focusing on the criterias we are targeting to redesign.

Digital Card Sorting

We evaluated and restructured the current information architecture of Department of Labor’s website, we used Miro for digital card sorting.

User Persona

After completing our research and developing a strong understanding of our user and her needs, we defined the persona that encompass Department of Labor user behaviors and needs.

Priortization Matrix

Used the data we collected and analyzed, we performed the “I wish, I like, what if...” technique and generated this priotization matrix and it’s clear that what’s important for our user.

Wireframes with InVision

After the we have all the information and research the team gather, I began the wireframe sketching down the main components that’s needed for this redesign. There were some A/B testings took place in this initial design stage to make sure this is the optimized layout for the users. The process began with ideation. rapidly sketch and ideate dozens of concepts for redesign, with combination of the best ideas into the first design iteration andtested for functionality and usability

Site Map

The restructure of the sitemap will help user to locate content easily and avoid many hidden links and steps, by doing this, we can reduce the negative and fustration emotion for our user while searching on the site.
